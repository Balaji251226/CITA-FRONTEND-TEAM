ABOUT US

Our Passion, Your Plate: "The Chicken Stall"

BRAND ORIGIN

The Chicken Stall started as a small family-owned stall in South India.

We perfected the art of crispy, flavorful fried chicken using traditional techniques and quality ingredients. Over the years, we have blended our heritage with global flavors, incorporating popular Indian tastes to create a unique and delicious experience.

Our secret lies in our special marinade and cooking method. We believe in hygiene and freshness, that every customer deserves a delicious, satisfying meal. Our spices are all hand-ground by us, so you can be sure of the freshness.

QUALITY AND SAFETY

Anytime you buy or eat chicken, you can be assured that the chicken is…

  • Free of added Hormones and Steroids
  • Raised Cage-Free
  • Monitored by Licensed Veterinarians
  • Raised by Farmers Trained in Animal Welfare
  • Monitored by Licensed Veterinarians

OUR COMMUNITY AND RESPONSIBILITY

"Food brings people together, and at Chicken Stall, we take that responsibility seriously. From supporting local suppliers to minimizing food waste, we are committed to making a difference. Whether it's through sustainable sourcing or community outreach, we strive to leave a lasting impact—one meal at a time."
